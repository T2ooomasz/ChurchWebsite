About Our Church

Serving our community with faith, hope, and love since 1974

Our History

1974

Foundation

Grace Community Church was founded by a small group of believers with a vision to create a welcoming spiritual home for all.

1985

First Building

We moved into our first permanent building, allowing us to expand our ministries and serve more families.

2000

Community Outreach

Launched our community outreach program, providing food, clothing, and support to those in need.

2020

Digital Ministry

Expanded our reach through online services and digital ministry programs.

Our Mission

To make disciples of Jesus Christ by sharing the Gospel, building a loving community, and serving others with compassion and grace.

Our Vision

To be a beacon of hope in our community, transforming lives through the love of Christ and creating lasting impact for God's kingdom.

What We Believe

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God, the ultimate authority for faith and practice.

We believe in one God, eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

We believe Jesus Christ is the Son of God, who died for our sins and rose again for our salvation.

We believe salvation is by grace through faith in Jesus Christ alone, not by works.

Our Services

Join us for worship, fellowship, and spiritual growth

Service Times

What to Expect

1

Warm Welcome

Our friendly greeters will welcome you at the door and help you find your way.

2

Contemporary Worship

Enjoy uplifting music that combines traditional hymns with modern worship songs.

3

Biblical Teaching

Hear relevant, Bible-based messages that apply to everyday life.

4

Children's Programs

Safe, fun, and educational programs for children of all ages during service.

5

Fellowship Time

Stay after service for coffee and conversation with our church family.

6

Come As You Are

Dress comfortably - we're more interested in your presence than your appearance.

Our Ministries

Children's Ministry

Engaging programs for kids from nursery through 5th grade, including Sunday School, VBS, and special events.

Youth Ministry

Dynamic programs for middle and high school students, featuring weekly gatherings, retreats, and mission trips.

Adult Education

Bible studies, small groups, and classes designed to deepen faith and build community.

Worship & Music

Opportunities to serve through choir, praise band, and technical arts ministries.

Outreach

Local and global mission opportunities to serve our community and spread the Gospel.

Prayer Ministry

Dedicated prayer teams and prayer meetings to lift up our church and community needs.
